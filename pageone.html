For Those Who Stay in Day 1 Mode Inspired by Jeff Bezos's Day 1 philosophy, which says that once you slip into Day 2, you start dying slowly.

What is Page One?

Page One is a close‑knit community of founders and solopreneurs in software and technology space who refuse to coast.

We meet regularly in Chennai over coffee to discuss what's working, what's not, and how to stay inventive even when the grind gets real.

Why Page One?

Every startup story begins with Page one where the idea is raw, the energy is high, and the world still feels possible. This group keeps that page open.
