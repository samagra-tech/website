Account Deletion Policy

This page outlines the steps to request account deletion for the Smart Save App, provided by Samagra Tech.

If you wish to delete your account, please follow the steps below:

  • Contact us at support@samagra.xyz with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  • Provide your account details, including the email address associated with your account.
  • We will verify your request and confirm the deletion within 7 business days.

Upon account deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • Personal information such as name, email, and account details.
  • Transaction history and associated data.

The following data may be retained for legal or compliance purposes for up to 90 days:

  • Audit logs and records of account deletion requests.
  • Data required for financial reporting or regulatory compliance.

For any questions or concerns regarding account deletion, please contact us at support@samagra.xyz.
